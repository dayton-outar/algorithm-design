# Definition

The Königsberg Bridge Problem is said to have been the origin of graph theory.

## Königsberg Bridge Problem

![Map of Königsberg](/.attachments/städte.königsberg-1255.jpg)

[Königsberg](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/K%C3%B6nigsberg) was the historic German name of the city that is now modern-day Kaliningrad, Russia. The city occupied two islands plus areas on both banks. These areas were linked together by seven bridges as shown in the map above and the illustration below. The citizens wondered whether they could leave home, cross every bridge exactly once, and return home. The problem reduces to traversing the figure on the right below, with heavy dots representing land masses and curves representing bridges.
